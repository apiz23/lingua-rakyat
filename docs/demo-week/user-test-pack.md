## Task For Testers

> Upload a government PDF and ask a question about it.

Do not explain the UI first. Watch where they hesitate.

## Observation Notes

For each tester, record:

- Did they understand where to upload?
- Did they understand when processing was done?
- Did they know what to ask?
- Did they notice the cited source/page?
- Did they trust the answer?
- Where did they get stuck?

## Questions To Ask After

1. What was helpful?
2. What confused you?
3. Would you use this for a real government document?
4. What language would you prefer?
5. What should be clearer before you trust the answer?

## Success Criteria

- At least 3 testers finish upload + question without help.
- At least 3 testers notice or understand citations.
- At least 1 useful quote can go into the pitch deck.
- Top 3 confusion points are identified.
